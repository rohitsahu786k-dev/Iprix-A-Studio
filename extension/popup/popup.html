How Auto works

● Auto on a template means it will auto-fill whenever you open a form in that category.

  • One per category. Turning Auto on for a template turns it off for others in the same category.
  • Fill (green button) always fills the form manually — Auto state doesn't affect it.
  • The top-right ● Auto is the global switch — turn it off to disable all auto-fills.
  • Single template in a category? You can still toggle Auto off — ○ Auto means it won't auto-fill.
